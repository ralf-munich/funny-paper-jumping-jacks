Funny Paper Jumping Jacks

Lithographs from the Wissembourg Printworks, 1880–1918

This website presents more than 150 original paper jumping jack lithographs from the Wissembourg printworks in Alsace, from the collection of Ralf Geissler. It is possibly one of the largest private collections dedicated to this field.
