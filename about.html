About the Collection

Funny Paper Jumping Jacks

Lithographs from the Wissembourg Printworks, 1880–1918

This website presents a private collection of more than 150 original paper jumping jack lithographs from the Wissembourg printworks in Alsace. Collected by Ralf Geissler, it is possibly one of the largest private collections dedicated to this field.
