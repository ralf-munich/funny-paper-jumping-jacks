Funny Paper Jumping Jacks

Lithographs from the Wissembourg Printworks, 1880–1918

This website presents original lithographed paper jumping jacks from the Wissembourg printworks in Alsace. The prints were acquired one by one over several years through the antiquarian market and are preserved here as a focused private collection.

The collection is devoted exclusively to Wissembourg. Its aim is not only to preserve charming examples of popular art and paper craftsmanship, but also to document a visual world shaped by theatre, folklore, costume, humour and historical imagination.

In scope, it is possibly one of the largest private collections of Wissembourg paper jumping jacks.
